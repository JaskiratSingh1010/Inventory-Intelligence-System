ℹ️ What is Inventory Adj.?
Inventory Adjustments (TransType 162) are manual corrections — e.g. stock revaluation (price changes without qty movement), year-end COGS journals, or discrepancy corrections. They appear in OINM but do not represent physical goods movement.
